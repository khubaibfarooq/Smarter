Multi-platform support

Smarter Guardians Proxy is available on all your devices, including PCs, Macs, smartphones, and even routers. A secure internet connection you can trust is essential to maintaining your privacy on your laptop at home, your mobile device on the road, or your workstation at the office. Smarter Guardians Proxy has native apps for Windows, macOS, Linux, Chromebook, Android, Android TV and iOS/iPadOS.
