The most important thing to evaluate when choosing a Proxy provider is whether it is trustworthy. This is crucial, as your Proxy provider handles your internet connection, meaning it can see the browsing history you’re trying to keep private.

Here are some questions you should ask:

1- Does the Proxy have a strict no-logs policy?

2- Is the Proxy based in a country with strong privacy laws?

3- Is the Proxy open source and independently audited?

Once you’re sure you can trust your Proxy provider, you can consider other features based on what you need the Proxy for, such as advanced security features, support for streaming, or ad blocking.
