How does a Proxy work?

When you use a Proxy, it essentially replaces your internet service provider and handles routing your internet connection. Here's how.

1

A Proxy connection links your device to a Proxy server run by a Proxy provider, like Smarter Guardians Proxy.

2

The connection between your device and the Proxy server is encrypted, preventing your internet service provider from seeing the contents of your data as it travels between the two.

3

The Proxy server sits between your internet service provider and the internet, blocking your ISP from seeing your online activity and preventing the websites you visit from seeing your real IP address.

4

When connected to a Proxy server in another country, you can bypass local censorship and effectively “spoof” your geographic location.
