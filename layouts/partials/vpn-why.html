Why use a proxy?

Secure your internet connection

A proxy provides privacy while surfing the net: from your internet service provider, your government, and the websites you visit.

Millions of people worldwide use proxy services as an invaluable tool to defeat censorship and access the open internet. It also protects you from “free” WiFi providers.

Keep your data private

Smarter Guardians Proxy is a community-driven proxy service that places the privacy of our users first and foremost.

We keep no logs, and what little data we do store is protected by Swiss data protection laws. All of our apps are open source and independently audited, and we support journalists and activists in the struggle for a free and open internet.

Smarter Guardians Proxy has thousands of secure proxy servers all around the world, including several free proxy servers.
