FAQ

Find the answers for the most frequently asked questions below

Is my billing information secure?

Absolutely! We work with top payment companies which guarantees your safety and security. All billing information is stored on our payment processing partner.

How can I cancel my subscription?

You can easily cancel your subscription at any time through your account. Once cancelled, you will not be charged for the following month.

What subscription options are available?

Currently, we only offer a monthly and yearly subscriptions. You have the flexibility to upgrade or cancel your monthly account at any time with no further obligations.

How can I update my payment information?

To update your payment information, simply go to the billing section of your dashboard and make the necessary changes.

Do you offer refunds?

Unfortunately no. We do not issue full or partial refunds for any reason.

Do you have a free plan?

Yes, we are delighted to offer a free plan for anyone who wishes to try out our service.
